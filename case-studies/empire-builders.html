The Challenge

Empire Builders UK was a highly skilled contracting firm with a website that didn't reflect their expertise. Their digital presence was a "silent brochure" — it looked generic, had no clear conversion paths, and failed to differentiate them from lower-tier competitors in the Bristol market.

The Strategy

Lune Studio implemented a full **Conversion Architecture** overhaul. We moved away from generic "About Us" content and focused on three psychological anchors:

  • Authority Signaling: Highlighting technical certifications and high-value project wins in the first 2 seconds of the scroll.
  • Frictionless Path: Replacing complex contact forms with a multi-channel booking system (WhatsApp + Strategy Call).
  • Technical Speed: Migrating to a globally distributed Jamstack framework to ensure 0.8s load times across the UK.

"Since Adhityan and the team stepped in, the phone hasn't stopped ringing. We literally doubled our conversion rate in the first week. They just understood the UK market."

— JOHN D., FOUNDER

The Outcome

Within 14 days of launch, the monthly lead volume moved from an average of 12 organic inquiries to 26. More importantly, the *quality* of leads improved, with high-ticket commercial inquiries making up 40% of the new mix.
