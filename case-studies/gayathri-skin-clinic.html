1. The Brief

Gayathri Skin Clinic is a premium dermatology hub in Chennai. Despite having world-class equipment and expertise, their digital presence was stuck in the "brochure era." They had high-intent patients searching for them, but they weren't capturing the demand.

2. Revenue Leaks Found

  • Invisible Procedures: High-value treatments like Laser and Chemical Peels were buried in deep menus.
  • The Booking Friction: Patients had to find a phone number, dial manually, and wait for a receptionist. 40% of mobile users dropped off here.
  • Generic Positioning: The site looked like a local clinic, not a premium aesthetic center, leading to price-sensitivity among potential clients.

3. The Lune Intervention

We didn't just redesign the site; we engineered a patient-capture machine.

  • Procedure-First Funnels: Created dedicated landing experiences for top-tier procedures with outcome-based imagery.
  • WhatsApp Direct Connect: Integrated a high-friction-to-low-friction booking bridge. One tap from mobile now starts a consultation.
  • Authority Inversion: Placed transformation proof and medical credentials at the very top of the hierarchy.

4. The ₹6L Result

Monthly revenue scaled from a stagnant ₹2L to a consistent ₹6L per month within 90 days of the new system launch. The clinic transitioned from chasing leads to managing a waitlist.

"The new system didn't just look better; it tripled our clinic's revenue in 90 days."
