The Challenge

Gayathri Skin Clinic, a premium dermatology and medical aesthetic center in Chennai, served high-profile clients but struggled with a digital presence that appeared "clinical" rather than "premium." Their patient acquisition was heavily dependent on word-of-mouth, which made consistent monthly revenue growth unpredictable.

The Strategy

We shifted the digital strategy from "Information" to "Intention." Most medical sites list services; we designed a system that visualizes outcomes.

  • Segmented Landing Experiences: We built procedure-specific funnels for high-value aesthetic treatments like Laser and Chemical Peels, rather than one general home page.
  • Trust Inversion: We prioritized outcome-based proof and verifiable patient transformations directly above the fold.
  • WhatsApp Integration: Since the Indian market operates on WhatsApp, we built a custom booking connector that tracked conversion attribution back to specific procedures.

"Our revenue literally tripled in 3 months. The new system doesn't just look better; it's a completely different machine for the clinic."

— DR. GAYATHRI, FOUNDER

The Outcome

Monthly aesthetic revenues scaled from ₹2L to consistently hitting ₹6L within 90 days. The clinic now operates at 85% capacity with a waitlist for specialized procedures, allowing them to shift from a "general clinic" model to a "specialist aesthetic hub."
